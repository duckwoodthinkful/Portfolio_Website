Hello, I am Abe Krebs

I live in Pennsylvania and I make laboratory equipment for use in electrochemistry. Electrochemistry covers all kinds of interesting topics, including batteries, corrosion, and solar cells. I bet you are using something every day that deals with electrochemistry!

About Abe

I attend Penn State University for both my undergraduate and graduate degrees in Engineering Science and Mechanics. I have been working for over 25 years in the field of electrochemistry. I have been writing code since I had my first computer, a CP/M based Televideo TS802.

I have worked with many technologies over the years. I can offer services in html, javascript, C/C++, C#, and LabView. I have also worked on developing and signing Windows device drivers. I love working with teams of people because together we can develop great things!
